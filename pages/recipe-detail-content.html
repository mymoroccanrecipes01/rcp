Essential Ingredients

For the crust
  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs (150 g)
  • 4 tbsp unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
For the filling
  • 2 cups Greek yogurt (full fat)
  • 1/2 cup sweetened condensed milk (or honey)
  • 1 cup crushed pineapple, drained
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
Notes
Use fresh pineapple if possible. For extra creaminess, fold in a little mascarpone.
Tip: Chill at least 2 hours before slicing for clean bars.

Detailed Instructions

  1. Prepare the crust: Mix graham crumbs, sugar and melted butter. Press firmly into a lined 9x9 pan. Bake 8‚Äì10 minutes until set. Let cool.
  2. Pineapple layer: Spread crushed pineapple evenly over the cooled crust.
  3. Yogurt cream: Whisk Greek yogurt, condensed milk and vanilla until smooth. Spread over pineapple layer.
  4. Chill: Refrigerate at least 2‚Äì4 hours until firm. Slice into 9‚Äì12 bars and serve.

Storage & Make Ahead

Store in an airtight container in the fridge for up to 4 days. Freeze for up to 1 month.
